Privacy Policy

Last updated July 21, 2026

The short version: Harrier runs on your own computer. Your résumé, your profile, your job searches, the jobs you apply to, and your API keys stay on your machine — we never receive them. The only information we get is what's needed to sell you a license and email it to you. Our website uses no cookies, analytics, or trackers.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Harrier ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Tyler Russell, a sole proprietor located in Minnesota, USA, collects and how we use it. By using Harrier or our website, you agree to this policy.

1. Our Approach: Local-First by Design

Harrier is software that runs locally on your computer. The application does its work on your machine and sends your application materials directly to the employers and platforms you choose, and (when you enable AI features) to Anthropic using your own API key. Because of this design, the personal data you put into Harrier does not pass through, and is not stored on, any server operated by us.

2. Information We Collect

We collect a deliberately small amount of information:

  • When you purchase a license: your name and email address, provided to us by our payment processor (Stripe) at checkout, along with a record of your purchase (what you bought and when). We never receive or store your full payment card number — Stripe handles payment details.
  • When we deliver your license and provide support: your email address (to send you your license key) and the contents of any support messages you send us.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about this. We do not receive, store, or have access to:

  • Your résumé, cover letters, or profile information;
  • The jobs you search for, view, or apply to;
  • The content of the applications Harrier submits on your behalf;
  • Your Anthropic API key or your AI usage; or
  • Any files on your computer.

All of the above stays on your machine and is transmitted only to the third parties you direct Harrier to interact with (employers, job platforms, and Anthropic).

4. Our Website

Our marketing website uses no cookies, no analytics, no advertising trackers, and no third-party scripts. We do not build a profile of your visit.

5. How We Use Information

We use the limited information described in Section 2 only to: deliver your license, provide customer support, keep records required for accounting and tax purposes, and prevent fraud or abuse. We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

6. Third Parties

  • Stripe processes your payment. Your payment information is handled under Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy.
  • Anthropic. When Harrier's AI features run, they use your own Anthropic account and API key. Your interactions with Anthropic are governed by your agreement with Anthropic, not by us.
  • Job platforms and employers. Harrier submits your applications directly to the platforms and employers you choose. Their handling of your application is governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain purchase and license records for as long as necessary to provide support and to meet legal, accounting, and tax obligations, and then delete or anonymize them. Support correspondence is kept only as long as useful for support.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you. Because we hold so little — essentially your email and a purchase record — these requests are simple. Email us at tyler.russell248@gmail.com and we will honor applicable requests. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

9. Children

Harrier is not directed to, and may not be used by, anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Contact us at tyler.russell248@gmail.com.

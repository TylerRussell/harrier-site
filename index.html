FAQ

The honest answers.

What does it cost?
In the beta it's free. You bring your own Anthropic API key, and the AI cost works out to roughly pennies per application — you pay Anthropic directly for exactly what you use, nothing to us.
What do I need to run it?
A Mac, Google Chrome, and an Anthropic API key. That's the whole list. Harrier runs locally and drives Chrome to do the applying.
Is it safe to let it apply for me?
It's built to be careful. Per-company pacing keeps it from ever spamming one employer, spend caps stop it from running away with cost, and you can switch on review-before-send whenever you want a human in the loop.
Can I review applications before they go out?
Yes. Human-assist mode fills in the entire application and then pauses on the submit step, so you get the final say on every one before it's sent.
How do I get access?
Harrier is in private beta right now. Email for an invite and you'll hear back when a spot opens.
